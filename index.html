The Story of the Vergil Chair

This website goes over the understandable history behind the meme of Vergil from Devil May Cry 5 sitting in a plastic chair

Wait, what's Devil May Cry 5 and Vergil Chair?

Devil May Cry 5 is a 2019 action-adventure game developed and published by Capcom. The game follows Nero, Dante, V, and Vergil as they stylishly fight off a demonic invasion. The character "Vergil" is the final boss of the game and the introduction to the fight involves him sitting in a demonic chair awaiting Dante's arrival before a final climactic duel. Little did Capcom know, this simple demonic chair would be turned into one of the most iconic memes of the entire franchise.

Origin of the Chair

Origin of the Chair Continued

It all started on September 23rd 2020. User "thezippotm" uploaded a mod for Devil May Cry 5 that changed Vergil's demonic chair (that he sits upon waiting for his climactic final duel with Dante) into an everyday plastic yard chair.

The same day, youtube user "Oatomiru" uploaded a video of this mod simply labeled "Vergil Chair". The absuridty of this normally extremely menacing and serious character sitting in a cheap plastic lawn chair would baffle and amuse the masses. As of time of writing, it has reached 2.7 Million views.
