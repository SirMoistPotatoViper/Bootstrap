The Story of the Vergil Chair

This website goes over the understandable history behind the internet meme of Vergil from Devil May Cry 5 sitting in a plastic chair

Wait, What's Devil May Cry 5 and Vergil Chair?

Devil May Cry 5 is a 2019 action-adventure game developed and published by Capcom. The game follows Nero, Dante, V, and Vergil as they stylishly fight off a demonic invasion. The character "Vergil" is the final boss of the game, and the introduction to the fight involves him sitting in a demonic chair awaiting Dante's arrival before a final climactic duel. Little did Capcom know, this simple demonic chair would be turned into one of the most iconic memes of the entire franchise.

Origin of the Chair

Origin of the Chair (continued)

It all started on September 23rd 2020. Nexusmods.com user "thezippotm" uploaded a mod for Devil May Cry 5 that changed Vergil's demonic chair (that he sits upon waiting for his climactic final duel with Dante) into a plastic yard chair.

The same day, youtube user "Oatomiru" uploaded a video of this mod simply labeled "Vergil Chair". The absuridty of this normally extremely menacing and serious character sitting in a cheap plastic lawn chair would baffle and amuse the masses. As of time of writing, the video has reached 2.7 Million views.

Rise of the Chair (continued)

The mod would quickly spike in popularity as further videos playing off the joke were formed. Videos such as having Vergil loudly slurp a drink mid-sentence or animating the scene and adding scraping noises to the chair. Both videos getting 1.3 million views at time of writing.

These videos would not only further boost the popularity of a meme, but get it to a sweet spot where people could twist the meme to their own liking. As more people found ways to adapt their humor, even more individuals would end up witnessing this meme. Many more videos of this type would go on to be made until the meme would reach it's peak form of evolution.

Rise of the Chair

The Chair Now

The Chair Now (continued)

The "Vergil Chair" meme then finally reached the climax of it's life-cycle. As users began to take the Veril Chair and remove it from just Devil May Cry 5. It would get expanded to other meme formats or concepts, such as "The Backrooms" from the SCP franchise, real life video footage of plastic chairs (typically overlayed with Vergil's theme "Bury the Light", which also experienced a similar journey in terms of meme cycles), a return to modding with people modding Vergil's plastic chair as a projectile, and even as a modded character to play as in games like "Rivals of Aether".

With this, the meme stood where it stands today. As an iconic meme among the Devil May Cry fandom and somewhat known outside of the fandom. This whole journey just goes to show how a little creativity can inspire thousands of people to create content to share. All of this, because somebody put Veril in a plastic chair.
